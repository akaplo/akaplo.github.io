About

I'm from a small-ish town in Massachusetts. Since I was young, computers always were my specialty, and I was the first contact for support by my family, friends, and eventually teachers. It was then that I decided that I wanted to spend my life building software that makes an impact on people's lives. I now study computer science at UMass Amherst! I consider myself a hacker, and I love to poke at systems and see where vulnerabilities are and incorporate what I learn into my own projects. It's been a slow start, but stay tuned for future projects that just might make a difference some day!
